## If Something Goes Wrong

### "I can't hear you"
- Make sure your **microphone is plugged in**
- Close other programs that use sound
- Speak **a little closer to your microphone**
- Refresh the webpage and try again

### "The website looks weird"
- This is normal! The assistant is **simplifying it for you**
- It removed ads and made text bigger
- It will go back to normal when you click "Stop"

### "I want to stop"
- Click the big red button **"Stop"**
- The website goes back to normal
- Click "Start Listening" to try again

### "I'm not sure what to say"
- Just **describe what you want to do naturally**
- The assistant understands regular English
- You don't need to use special commands
- Examples:
  - "I need to pay my electric bill"
  - "Show me pictures of beaches in Hawaii"
  - "Help me book a flight to see my grandkids"

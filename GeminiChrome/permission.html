⚠️ Permission Not Granted

If you don't see a browser permission dialog, or if you accidentally dismissed it, try these steps:

  1. Click the extension icon in Chrome (top right, puzzle piece icon)
  2. Click the three dots menu next to "Web Assistant"
  3. Select "Manage extension"
  4. Go to "Permissions" tab
  5. Enable "Microphone" access
  6. Close this window and try again

Or simply paste this in your address bar: chrome://extensions/
